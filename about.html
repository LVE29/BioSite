About Dane

Dane is someone who values hard work, loyalty, and steady progress. He is currently completing an electrician apprenticeship, a path that makes sense for him because he enjoys hands on learning and practical problem solving. A big influence in his life has been his dad, who taught him the importance of learning skills that are useful in the real world and taking pride in doing a job the right way. Over time, Dane has grown into someone who is dependable, focused, and willing to put in the effort to build a strong future.

Outside of work, Dane stays busy with the things that matter most to him: family, friends, and experiences. He is a dedicated sports fan, especially when it comes to baseball.
